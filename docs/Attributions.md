# The Grand Library: Scroll of Attributions

This document serves as the formal record of the visual and conceptual assets 
powering the **Ptah Network** and **Thoth's Notebook**.

## I. Sacred Iconography
* **Seshat Vector Trace**: Based on the New Kingdom reliefs at the Temple of Luxor. 

* **Iphty Link Icon - small **: Heiroglyph Y1 By George Douros - Rendered by User:Vorziblix from George Douros’s ‘Abydos Bold’ font., Public Domain, https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=73545062

* **Iphty Link Icon - Large **: Fragment of pavement depicting ducks in papyrus marsh - Gallery number: 3 – Ground Floor; Period: New Kingdom, Amenophis IV; Dynasty: 18th Dynasty; Place of discovery: Tell El-Amarna; Size: H. 100 cm W. 160 cm; Material: Plaster https://egyptianmuseumcairo.eg/artefacts/fragment-of-pavement/ - suggested attribution: ### IV. Historical References & Inspiration
* ** Iphty-Flyer Icon and Animation Reference**: 
    * *Source*: [The Egyptian Museum Cairo](https://egyptianmuseumcairo.eg/artefacts/fragment-of-pavement/)
    * *Subject*: Fragment of pavement depicting ducks in a papyrus marsh (Tell El-Amarna, 18th Dynasty).
    * *Usage*: Used as a geometric and anatomical reference for the **Iphty-Link** vector animations. 
    * *Note*: This asset is a modern digital derivation of ancient public domain funerary/palace art.


* **The Neheh-Circuit**: Original design by Google Gemini with custom vector graphics by Ibis Labs, inspired by the *Shen* ring.

* **The  (Inbox Icon)**: Standard Y3 Hieroglyph from Gardiner's List.
